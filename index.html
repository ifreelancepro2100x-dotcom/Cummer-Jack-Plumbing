Problems We Solve Daily

Common Plumbing Issues Jacksonville Homeowners Face

Florida's climate and Jacksonville's unique geology create plumbing challenges you will not find in other parts of the country. High humidity accelerates pipe corrosion, sandy shifting soil stresses underground lines, and mineral-heavy water leaves scale deposits inside fixtures and appliances. Here are the problems our plumbing team resolves most often — and the affordable solutions we bring to each one.

Skyrocketing Water Bills From Hidden Leaks

A single undetected leak behind a wall or beneath your slab can waste thousands of gallons per month. Our plumbers use acoustic sensors and infrared cameras for precise water leak detection and slab leak detection — locating the exact point of failure without tearing apart your home.

Slow Drains & Recurring Sewer Backups

Root intrusion from Jacksonville's mature live oaks and accumulated grease are the top causes of chronic drain issues. We attack them with professional drain cleaning, high-pressure hydro jetting, and sewer camera inspections to confirm the line is completely clear before we leave.

Inconsistent Hot Water or Complete Failure

Water heaters in Jacksonville's hard-water environment suffer sediment buildup that shortens their lifespan. Whether you need a quick water heater repair, a full tank replacement, or a modern tankless water heater upgrade, we handle it same-day with a free installation estimate.

Rusty Water & Failing Pipe Systems

Many Jacksonville homes — especially in Riverside, Springfield, Ortega, and San Marco — still run on original galvanized or polybutylene piping. When rust appears at the tap or pinhole leaks multiply, a whole-house repipe with modern PEX or copper eliminates the problem permanently and often increases property value.

Affordable Solutions

Budget-Friendly Plumbing Services Across Jacksonville FL

Affordable does not mean cutting corners. Every repair and installation performed by Cummer Jack Plumbing meets Florida Building Code and comes with written documentation. We simply keep overhead low, price honestly, and let our craftsmanship earn your repeat business.

Everyday Fixture Repairs

Dripping faucets, running toilets, and leaky showerheads waste water and money. Our plumbers carry parts for most major brands and complete faucet and sink repairs, toilet repairs, and shower valve replacements in a single visit — no second trip needed.

Trenchless Sewer Solutions

Traditional sewer excavation destroys driveways, landscaping, and budgets. Our trenchless sewer line repair creates a brand-new pipe inside the damaged one — saving your yard, your time, and up to $999 with our current promotion. We verify results with a post-repair camera inspection.

Water Quality Improvements

Jacksonville's water leaves white residue on dishes, dries out skin, and shortens appliance lifespans. A properly sized water softener or filtration system eliminates hardness minerals and chlorine taste at an affordable monthly cost that pays for itself in appliance longevity.

Kitchen & Bathroom Upgrades

Renovating on a budget? We handle the plumbing side of any kitchen remodel or bathroom remodel — rough-in relocations, supply line extensions, bathtub swaps, tub-to-shower conversions, and dishwasher hookups.

Gas Line & Boiler Service

Gas appliances demand precision. Our EPA Lead-Safe and ASSE-certified plumbers perform gas line installation, repair, and leak testing along with boiler repair and replacement — fully permitted and inspected for your safety.

Storm & Flood Prevention

Hurricane season and Jacksonville's flat topography put basements and crawl spaces at risk. We install and service sump pumps, backflow prevention devices, and water line connections to protect your property before the next big storm rolls in from the Atlantic.
